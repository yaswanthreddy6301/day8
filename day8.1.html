chicken dum biryani

Rayalaseema chicken biryani is a popular dish from the Rayalaseema region in Andhra Pradesh, India. Here is a recipe for making Rayalaseema chicken biryani:
Ingredients:
1 kg chicken, cut into medium-sized pieces
4 cups basmati rice
4 onions, finely chopped
2 cups yogurt
2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
3-4 green chillies, slit
2-3 bay leaves
4-5 cardamom pods
4-5 cloves
1-inch cinnamon stick
1 tsp shahi jeera (caraway seeds)
1 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp red chilli powder
2 tsp biryani masala powder
Salt to taste
Oil or ghee
Water
For garnish:
Fried onions
Chopped coriander leaves
Chopped mint leaves
Saffron strands, soaked in milk
